YALA National park















    Flora And Fauna In Yala




    • Flora:
      • Lagoons, estuaries, coral reefs, sea grass beds, salt marshes, mangroves.

      • 2004 tsunami caused damage, but vegetation was resilient and recovered.

      • Climate change causing spread of woody weeds.


    • Fauna:
      • Diverse wildlife like leopards, elephants, birds.

      • Minimal long-term tsunami impact on fauna.
